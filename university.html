Welcome to PIEAS

Nestled in the serene setting of a beautiful countryside, Pakistan Institute of Engineering and Applied Sciences (PIEAS), Islamabad, is recognized as a preeminent federal public university devoted to higher education in engineering, natural sciences, and formal sciences.

The Institute has been consistently ranked No. 1 for Engineering & Technology by the Higher Education Commission (HEC), Pakistan. With our highly qualified faculty and excellent infrastructure, we aim to contribute to the welfare of humanity through research and development.
